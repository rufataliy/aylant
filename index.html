Motomed VIVA 2

The MOTOmed viva2 brings professional movement therapy to your home. SmoothShapes XV

The SmoothShapes XV device uses the Company's proprietary Photomology® platform: a process that treats cellulite by combining 915 continuous wave diode laser and 650 LED energy along with mechanical manipulation (vacuum and massage).
